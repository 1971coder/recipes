# A Doctor’s Favourite Salad

A fresh, satisfying salad with crispy halloumi, spiced chickpeas and a bright lemon–honey dressing.

---

## Ingredients

### Salad
- 300 g halloumi, cut into cubes  
- 570 g chickpeas (1 large tin), drained and rinsed  
- 2 tbsp hot honey  
- 1 tsp chilli flakes  
- 1 tsp paprika  
- 1/2 cucumber, finely chopped  
- 3 beef tomatoes, finely chopped  
- 1/2 red onion, finely chopped  
- 1 yellow pepper, finely chopped  
- Large handful of fresh coriander, finely chopped  

### Dressing
- Juice of 1 lemon  
- 1 tbsp honey  
- 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil  
- Salt and pepper, to taste  

---

## Method

1. **Cook the halloumi**  
   Heat a little oil in a pan over medium heat.  
   Add the halloumi cubes and cook until golden and crispy on all sides.  
   Remove from heat and set aside.

2. **Spice the chickpeas**  
   Preheat oven to 200°C (fan).  
   Spread chickpeas on a lined baking tray.  
   Add hot honey, chilli flakes and paprika, then toss well.  
   Roast for 20–25 minutes until golden and slightly crisp.

3. **Prepare the vegetables**  
   In a large bowl, combine cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, yellow pepper and coriander.

4. **Make the dressing**  
   Whisk together lemon juice, honey, olive oil, salt and pepper.

5. **Assemble**  
   Add the roasted chickpeas and halloumi to the vegetables.  
   Pour over the dressing and toss gently to combine.

6. **Serve**  
   Serve warm or at room temperature.

---

## Notes

- Swap hot honey for regular honey if you prefer less heat.  
- Keeps well for lunch the next day (store dressing separately if possible).  
- Great as a main or a side with grilled chicken or fish.
